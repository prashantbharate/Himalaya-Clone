SHIPPING & RETURNS

SHIPPING

HOW MUCH DOES SHIPPING COST?

Orders under $35 will incur a $10 shipping fee. Orders over $35 ship FREE.

CAN I CHANGE OR CANCEL MY ORDER BEFORE IT HAS SHIPPED?

We try to pack and send your order as quickly as possible, which may limit our ability to edit your order or prevent a package from shipping. Reach out to us at writetous@himalayausa.com with a specific request, and we’ll do our best to accommodate.

WHEN WILL MY ORDER ARRIVE?

All orders are processed within 3 business days and should arrive within 5 business days after being shipped. An email with tracking information will be sent once your order has shipped.

DO YOU SHIP OVERSEAS?

This website only offers domestic US shipping. International friends, please check here for help finding Himalaya outside of the US.

WHAT METHOD DO YOU USE TO SHIP?

Most orders will ship via USPS. Occasionally, an order may ship via FedEx.

RETURNS PROCESS

WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

For over 90 years, the name Himalaya has been synonymous with trust in the world of wellness. We carefully formulate our products with the utmost care and stand behind the full line with a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you’re unhappy for any reason, please let us know so that we can help you return your products within 60 days of ordering for a full refund. This policy is only applicable for products purchased from us directly at himalayausa.com. If you purchased from a different retailer, please refer to their return policy.

HOW LONG DO I HAVE TO RETURN THE PRODUCT?

60 days from the purchase date.

HOW DO I START MY RETURN?

Please reach out to our customer service team at 1-800-869-4640 or writetous@himalayausa.com.

WHAT IF MY PRODUCT IS DAMAGED?

If the product arrived damaged, please contact us at writetous@himalayausa.com and attach images as needed. Our team is happy to make things right via a replacement product or refund.

WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY REFUND?

Once the returned product is received at our warehouse, you will be credited to the original form of payment within 7-10 business days.

IS THERE A RETURN FEE?

We charge you no return fee for any product that arrives damaged, however, if you are requesting a general return (i.e. you changed your mind and no longer want the product) an $8 restocking fee will be applied and deducted from your refund.

CAN I RETURN A PRODUCT PURCHASED SOMEWHERE OTHER THAN THIS WEBSITE?

No, we can only accept returns for products purchased directly from himalayausa.com. If you purchased your item from another website or retail store, please contact them directly to understand their return and refund policy./p>
