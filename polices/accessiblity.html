Website Accessibility Policy

Himalaya Herbal Healthcare is committed to ensuring that its website is accessible to people with disabilities. All the pages on our website will meet The Department of Health and Human Services checklist to the best of our abilities and have been tested through WebAIM. Any issues should be reported to info@himalayausa.com. The following functionality is currently implemented as of July 1st, 2017:

ALT TAGS: All video, images, audio files, and plugins will have Alt Tags

LINK DESTINATIONS: If an image or CTA links to another page, then Alt Tags and Title Tags will indicate where the user will be going.

DESCRIPTIONS AND HEADERS: All videos and images will include descriptions. All header tags will be placed in order (H1, H2, H3).

FORMS: When form controls are text input fields, we will use the LABEL element. Form fields are in a logical tab order.

IMAGES & EFFECTS: No page will contain repeatedly flashing images. No page will contain a strobe effect.

SKIP NAVIGATION: All pages will have ability to skip navigation for screen reader use.
